APEX RUN 2026

Apex Run 2026 is a national level vibrant community marathon hosted by Milagres College, Mangaluru, in association with ICYM Mangalore and Mangalore Runners Club. Scheduled for 10th May 2026, the event brings together over 2500 participants including students, athletes, and fitness enthusiasts to promote health, endurance, and unity. With multiple race categories designed for all fitness levels, Apex Run aims to inspire an active lifestyle while strengthening community spirit and sports culture in the region.

About Milagres College

Milagres College, Mangaluru, established in 1848, is one of the oldest and reputed institutions in the region. Managed by the Catholic Board of Education and affiliated to Mangalore University, the college offers quality higher education with a focus on academic excellence, holistic development, and values-based learning.

GUIDELINES

  1. The event includes 10 KM and 5 KM competitive runs, and a 3 KM fun run.
  2. All participants must register in advance and wear their bib number visibly during the race.
  3. Winners will be awarded separately in Men’s and Women’s categories for 5 KM and 10 KM.
  4. 1st Place: Cash Prize + Trophy
  5. 2nd Place: Cash Prize + Smaller Trophy
  6. Winners may also receive vouchers in addition to prizes.
  7. The 3 KM run is a non-competitive fun run, and no prizes will be awarded.
  8. A Lucky Bib Prize will be given to one participant selected randomly.
  9. A Reel Contest will be conducted; details will be shared separately, and winners will receive a cash prize.
  10. Participants must follow the route and instructions given by organizers and volunteers.
  11. Any misconduct or cheating will result in disqualification.
  12. Participants must ensure they are medically fit to participate.
  13. The organizer’s decision will be final in all matters.
